About us

Marial art gallery was founded in 2021 and it is based in
Nairobi ,Kenya.Our intention is to link the art lovers and the curators.We
aim to create a strong and sustainable art market to all artists
across Kenya and around the globe. Our gallery increases vsibility
for both established and emerging artists .Our goal is to be the
number one online plartform through provocking multi-disciplinary art across the globes

Various types of paintings we showcase.

Pastel

Their stick form and lack of drying time make
them an easy and portable solution for artists.

Enaustic

This complex technique dates back to ancient
Egypt and involves adding pigment to hot beeswax.

Gouache

This type of paint dries matte and is a heavier
paint due to the chalk that's incorporated into it.

Oil

This slow drying medium is made from color
pigments suspended in oil.

Acrylic

This water-soluble paint dries quickly, is non-toxic,
and cost-effective, often making it a go-to choice
for painters of all skill levels.

Watercolor

Watercolors are made from pigments suspended
in a water-based solution.
